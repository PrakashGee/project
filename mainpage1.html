PRAKASH SUPER MARKET
About us
Prakash Super Market is a 22 Years old super market with over 35 branches in South India. Prakash Super Market is known for its quality and is recognized as a leader in providing customer needs.

Vision and Mission

• To be in the forefront of customer needs and make emerging products reach the major segment of the society.
• Providing quality products across 1 billion Indian populations.
• Our mission is to open the floodgates of products offer to everybody. As a part of its mission, it intends to take our supplier to the remotest corner of the Tamil Nadu and slowly & steadily in rest of India.

Quality Policy
Our Aim is to sell products according to the needs of the customer by imparting on the emerging products quickly and uniformly in all the branches at an optimum cost. We commit wholeheartedly to satisfy the customer expectations as well as to continually improve the effectiveness of the Quality Management System through planned efforts and involvement of all personnel.
